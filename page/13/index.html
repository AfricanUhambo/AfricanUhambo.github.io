Summer 2008 - France & Spain

September 01, 2008
In 2007 we decided on our Big African Adventure trip. We had made the decision to put our flat on the market to fund the trip. Dan and I decided that 8 years together, one daughter later and the beginning of a life changing trip was a good moment to get married. We broke the news to our parents in the November of 2007 at Cassie's birthday. 'The Big One' was going to be a sort of familymoon.
