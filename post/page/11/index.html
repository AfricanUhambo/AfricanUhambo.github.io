DAY 5/6: WEDNESDAY 3/4 AUGUST 2011, Bürserberg (Austria) – Dovje (Slovenia)

August 05, 2011
Camping Costs: Camp Kamne €50.16 incl. electric – 2 nights Today was a long drive. We set off a little later than we should have and encountered lots of windy mountain passes. DAY 16: 22 AUGUST,Teplice nad Metuji – Ĉeský Krumlov

August 23, 2010
We woke early today. Breaking camp and packing up was easier with our wonderful new tents. Today we are off to Ĉeský Krumlov, stopping along the way at the Church of Bones in Kostince near Kutná Hora. We were traffic lucky today and made it to Kostnice in good time. The church is the outcome of an estimated 40 000 to 70 000 peoples bones used to decorate the church. The church is said to be a reminder of our humanity, that without redemption we cannot enter heaven.
