Blog Moved Home Again

Moving from Wordpress.com to Github

Posted by us on Sat, Jan 14, 2017
In General
Tags blog

Having previously moved from Google pages to Wordpress.com. The Blog (this site) has now moved from Wordpress.com to a statically served site hosted on Github.com.

The majority of the posts are now moved and most content is running correctly. Over the next few weeks I’ll tweak and fine tune the content and appearance. Previous links should still work, I hope!

I thought I would deploy at this stage despite needing to finish a few bits, to ensure continuity. As an old boss said:

Don’t let perfect be the enemy of good…

If you are interested in the technical details of moving from the Wordpress.com platform to a static site, it will be published in the coming days on TechNotes.
