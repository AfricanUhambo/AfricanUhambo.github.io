Needed a lightweight stove

June 23, 2013
On Friday, I needed to get a lightweight stove for a quick camping microadventure on Saturday night. I did not want to buy one and was considering self heating meals and no coffee in the morning. Until I found inspiration from a post on twitter to a video by Tom Allen. I decided to have a go at building my own also. I had some mentholated spirit for fuel, I just needed a drink can to make the stove.
