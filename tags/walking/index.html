Ford Railway Station to Devils Dyke Walk

March 19, 2012
Wendy and I, have recently walked from Ford railway station to Devils Dyke, we followed the Arun river up-stream past Arundel to Amberley, were we spent the night. From there we walked along the South Downs Way to Devils Dyke. We spent the night in Amberley which is a fantastic little village in Sussex. DAY 14/15: 20/21 AUGUST Martin (Slovakia) – Teplice nad Metuji (Czech Republic)

August 22, 2010
Camping costs: 560 Crowns – 2 nights = €23.00 After an initial hiccup of taking the wrong exit and having to do a big loop, we finally made our way to the Czech. Unfortunately for us the road was VERY slow going. Not a motorway, still a major road, but FULL of trucks and slow moving vehicles making it a long journey. We stopped at 12pm for lunch and a drink, but we were still stuck at about 2pm in a traffic jam with still roughly 90km to go till we get to Adršpach.
