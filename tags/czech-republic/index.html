Some Pictures from the Czech Republic - Prague

February 23, 2013
In February we took 6 days out, to visit our friends Kamil and Helena, who were taking a holiday from their overland trip driving from Hove to Australia. The reason for their break was because the car was being shipped from Japan to Australia. Need less to say it was freezing. We spent the first day in Prague, then went to Jihlava to see their home town and then returned to Prague for another day.

DAY 16: 22 AUGUST,Teplice nad Metuji – Ĉeský Krumlov

August 23, 2010
We woke early today. Breaking camp and packing up was easier with our wonderful new tents. Today we are off to Ĉeský Krumlov, stopping along the way at the Church of Bones in Kostince near Kutná Hora. We were traffic lucky today and made it to Kostnice in good time. The church is the outcome of an estimated 40 000 to 70 000 peoples bones used to decorate the church. The church is said to be a reminder of our humanity, that without redemption we cannot enter heaven.

DAY 14/15: 20/21 AUGUST Martin (Slovakia) – Teplice nad Metuji (Czech Republic)

August 22, 2010
Camping costs: 560 Crowns – 2 nights = €23.00 After an initial hiccup of taking the wrong exit and having to do a big loop, we finally made our way to the Czech. Unfortunately for us the road was VERY slow going. Not a motorway, still a major road, but FULL of trucks and slow moving vehicles making it a long journey. We stopped at 12pm for lunch and a drink, but we were still stuck at about 2pm in a traffic jam with still roughly 90km to go till we get to Adršpach.
