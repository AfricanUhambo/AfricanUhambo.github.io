5 Minutes jobs are never 5 minutes

May 19, 2013

Why is it every job on my Landy that should be five minutes, takes hours!

Changed the front mud flaps on the Land Rover yesterday... and today. Passenger side easy.

Drivers side was a different story. One of the bolts was heavily corroded into the rivnut. This meant that the rivnut spun... Only choice was to rip the rivnut out and replace it. Luckily I managed to fit a new one of the same size. All without the proper tool to clamp it.

Nice trick with a bolt, washer and nut. Let me know if you can't figure it out.

Anyway another five minute job done in 2 hours.
