Almost There Upstand Assembly

Posted by us on Fri, Jul 19, 2013
In Defender, Land Rover, Maintenance, Preparation, Summer 2013
Tags defender, land rover, maintenance, preparation, seat brackets

Not much of an update tonight, with the ongoing repair apart from to show some pictures of the panel now it has been fully tweaked and drilled to fit.

I finished of the drilling tonight and deburred everything.

Final job for the night was to give another coat of etch primer and paint the seat brackets and new panels with stone chip paint.

They are now drying, so tomorrow I'll start fitting it for real. The only thing I need to do is drill the seat brackets in location.

I am hopeful with a push, I should have it done tomorrow or Sunday at the latest. Not wishing to tempt fate!
