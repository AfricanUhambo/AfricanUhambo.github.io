Some Stats - Summer 2012 Trip

Posted by us on Tue, Apr 16, 2013
In Defender, Summer 2012, Travel, Wild Camping
Tags defender, europe, free camping, maintenance, overland, travel, wild camping

In starting to consider this years plan, i thought i would post some recorded figures from last summer.

Some stats from our last trip from 27th July to the 26th August 2012.

  • We covered 3663.1 miles, about 5900 km.
  • We used 606.42 litres of diesel.
  • Averaged 27 miles per gallon.
  • Filled up 15 times.
  • Paid 26 tolls totalling 287.8 Euro.
  • The most expensive where for the Tunnel de Mont Blanc at 38.9 and tunnel de Frejus at 39.8
  • We used the channel tunnel for crossing the channel
  • We used a over night ferry from Bari to Dubrovnik
  • 1 night in a hotel.
  • 2 attempted nights at a hotel night resulting in six police officers attending.
  • 4 nights free camping
  • 1 none catastrophic car part failure.
