A Visit to the Map Shop - Stanfords

Posted by us on Sat, Apr 27, 2013
In Maps, Planning, Preparation, Summer 2013, Wild Camping
Tags london, maps, planning, stanfords

We found ourselves at a loose end this morning, the weather was iffy. So we took a pleasant ride into London for a wonder.

We are considering are plans for the summer so decided we'd pop to the map shop in London. Those of you who don't know it. This is probably the best place to purchase maps and travel books in London. It has three floors of books and maps normally in the scale you need.

We got a Bradt Albania Guide, a 1:150k Kosovo map, 1:200k Albania map, 1:400 Bulgaria map and 1:500k Romania / Moldova map, the latter two are not ideal scales for what we need but they'll do. I'll probably get better scales if I can find them. Unfortunately I could not find a decent Macedonia map so had to resort to Amazon for that and a Kosovo and Macedonia guide book.

If you get the chance you should pop in, it is a pretty cool shop!
