African Uhambo

Day 14 - Kostanjica

August 09, 2013 Summer 2013, travel

We took it easy today, not a lot to say. We woke up late, had a slow breakfast and coffee. Caught up on laundry and swam in the sea. At lunch time we headed back to the campsite to get out the sun and fell asleep in the shade. About 1600 we headed back to the beach for another swim.

We chatted with people, our neighbours and had a generally good time.

In the evening we had dinner at a cool restaurant behind the town which was another little paradise set in an old mill. Cassie had loads of fun exploring while we waited for dinner.

Tomorrow we head south.
