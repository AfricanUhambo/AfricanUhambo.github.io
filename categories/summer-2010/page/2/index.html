DAY 4: 10 AUGUST, Tübingen (Germany) – Altenmarkt (Austria)

August 11, 2010
Camping costs: €21.00 – 1 night We set off for Austria and a camp that Pete had seen on the net and added to his GPS. We saw a Lidl on the way and decided to stop for food for that evening. We got snacks, beer, lunch, chocolate, fruit and dinner all for a mere €26.83. Much better price! The German motorways all signal their exits with a sign saying AUSFARHT.

DAY 2 & 3: 8/9 AUGUST 2010, Neufchâteau (Belgium) – Tübingen (Germany)

August 09, 2010
Camping costs: €53.00 – 2 nights As soon as we had packed up and hit the road, the wet set in and continued to stay wet right the way across into Germany. A day for border crossings, wonder what it would have been like if we had had to go through the customs as we went into France, Luxembourg, Germany, France again and back into Germany to reach Tübingen. We are meeting up with Pete in Tübingen at the camp site there on the River Nekar.

DAY 1: 7 AUGUST 2010, Calais (France) - Neufchâteau (Belgium)

August 08, 2010
Camping costs: €17.30 - 1 night Early start, we set out at 02.45 hrs for Dover to catch our 05.55 hrs ferry to Calais. Pretty uneventful crossing with no major hiccups and actually remembering to drive on the other side of the road. We headed out on the A16 to Dunkerque and then down into Belgium. We had hardly realised that we had come into Belgium until we suddenly saw the border crossing sign.
