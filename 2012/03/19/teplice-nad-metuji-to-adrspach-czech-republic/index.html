Teplice nad Metuji to Adrspach - Czech Republic

March 19, 2012 Czech Republic, Summer 2010

While we where in the Czech Republic in August 2010, during our summer holidays we stayed in Teplice Nad Metuji, this is part of a National Park around the Adrspach area, and is a very nice place to visit and walk.

The park its self is home to sandstone formations that have been paired with famous monuments and people in name, it covers 17 km square in the Bohemia area of the Northern Czech Republic.

All the trails are really well marked out and walking maps are easily available we had a 1:16000 map which was great.
