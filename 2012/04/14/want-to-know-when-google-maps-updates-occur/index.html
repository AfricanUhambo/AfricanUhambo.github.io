Want to know when Google Maps updates occur?

Posted by us on Sat, Apr 14, 2012
In Overland, Planning
Tags google maps, navigation, planning

Doing my usual this morning browsing the threads on Horizons Unlimited, I spotted a thread that is about a new Google maps update notification service, which is something I have longed for, for a long time. This new service from Google will alter you when maps are updated in an area you choose. It is called "Follow Your World"

If you are a regular gmaps user you are likely to have spotted that there is still quite a bit that still needs to be "done". Last year I noticed that Bosnia and Herzegovina was missing detail of the road routing for instance.
