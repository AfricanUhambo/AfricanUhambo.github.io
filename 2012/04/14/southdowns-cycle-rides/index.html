Southdowns Cycle Rides

April 14, 2012 Mountain Biking, South Downs Way, Sussex

A couple of years ago I managed to get round to getting myself a new Mountain bike, a Boardman if you are interested she is on the right there and rather filty, they are great value. Since then I have been trying to do as many of the tracks on my local Ordnance Survey map as I can.

I have not got round to putting down all of the info on the rides I have been doing locally, but you can see them on Everytrail. I have spent the winter trying to do every track I can on the Ordnance Survey 1:25000 map for Brighton.

Also you'll find the GPS tracks for the Afan Forest rides we did. Awesome rides, you should go if you get the chance. Make sure you and your bike are fit!

I hope someone finds the tracks useful / interesting!
